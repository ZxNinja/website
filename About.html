Rise Up Coffee Co.

About Us

Since 1938 our ancestors are manufacturing coffee powder. Rise up coffee was started in Chickmagalur in Karnataka which is globally recognised as Coffee Bowl of India. Decades of manufacturing experience has made us expertise in coffee industry and well known for the quality and consistency. Rise up coffee has got wide range of products with different blends of roasted coffee seeds and grown in the lush coffee plantations of Chickmagalur in Karnataka India.

Rise up coffee is unique from others because India’s only filter coffee using German machine to roast and grind both coffee and chicory in the coffee industry. Rise up coffee powder is manufactured out of specially picked coffee beans and chicory picked by a team of experts to maintain quality of the product and for its long time sustainability in market. Coffee beans used in our powder are high altitudes coffee which are grown above 5000 ft sea level.

Rise up coffee is maintaining the standard for quality and we have equipped with latest machinery from roasting, grinding and packing to maintain consistency for quality and safety parameters. Rise up Coffee is supported by dedicated channel partners who are pioneers in retail marketing like D-Mart, Namdhari’s Fresh, Simpli Namdhari’s, Adhishwar’s, DMart, Big Bazaar, Nilgiris, Heritage, Easyday, Hypercity, More etc. Apart from leading players in the Hotels and Hospitality Industry, online channel partners Bigbasket, Amazon, Flipkart, Grofers, Flavour of my city. Rise up Coffee Company is managed by dedicated Directors who are experts in their respective fields.
